Family Sauce

This sauce was done by Jose Sang. Here you will learn how to do our special family sauce wich you can use for almost anything. Originally this was done for fried rice, but you can use it for whatever you like. Enough about the story, let's teach you how to do it. In this case for a family of 4.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Paprika
  • Mayonnaise

Preparation

  1. Smash 2 tooth of garlic and leave it on a side for later.
  2. Take out a pan and start heating it on a medium level.
  3. Insert 2 spoons of butter in the pan.
  4. Insert the garlic you smashed before on the pan and start mixing it with the butter.
  5. Now start adding and mixing some paprika until almost everything is red.
  6. At this point you can leave the pan with the fire on a very low level.
  7. Take out the mayonnaise and take the amount you think it is enough for a family of 4.
  8. Turn off the fire heating the pan.
  9. Now slowly insert with a spoon a little of the sauce in the pan and mix it with the mayonnaise you took out.
  10. Repeat step 9 until There is nothing else to take out from the pan.
  11. That's it! Now you can taste it and leave it in the refrigerator for later use
